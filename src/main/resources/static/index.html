Bridging the gap between college students and qualified peer tutors.

About us

As current teaching assistants we noticed that while there are resources provided by schools like free tutoring and office hours, there are multiple students with many time commitments and conflicting events that prevent them from taking advantage of these resources that would help them academically. Tutor Bridge is aimed to solve this by connecting tutors with flexible schedules to students in order to accommodate to their needs.
